PlutoPay

PlutoPay is and E-Wallet app that lets the user send and receive money, invest, and manage. All in one convenient place.

Role

Research, UX and UI.

Tools

Pen and Paper, Balsamiq, Adobe XD, LucidChart, Usability Hub.

Timeline

Dec 2020 - May 2021

User Research

One of the first steps was to conduct user research and user interviews to better understand the need, goals, and overall behavior of my potential users. As well as identify any pain points and challenges they may be facing using e-wallet and investing apps to manage their finances. I asked questions about the needs and golas of the users, any challenges they may face with investing and money management, pain points with past experiences using e-wallet or investing apps, and about general feeling towards the use of e-wallet and investing apps.

User Personas, Journey Maps & User Flows

After conducting my user research, I created two user personas to help me to maintain empathy with the potential users during the design process. I created journey maps for the users to help me get a feel about how they would go about completing specific tasks within the app. And the user flows helped me to make the design more user centered so that users could complete tasks as quickly and efficiently as possible with little to no confusion.

Wireframes and Prototype

After creating user personas, journey maps, and user flows I begam creating wireframes for the app. I started off with low-fidelity wireframes using pen and paper then went into mid-fidelity using Balsamiq, and Adobe XD. From there I conducted usability testing and got user feedback to start working on the polished high-fidelity wireframes and prototypes for the app.

Usability Testing

After completing a clickable prototype, I conducted usability tests with potential users. I wrote a test script and had users perform a series of tasks within the prototype. Through these tests I was able to identify issues that needed to be fixed in order to make the tasks easier and more user friendly. One of the main issues I found was having to many steps and screen when trying to send a payment or deposit accrued balance. I was able to do a quick redesign to make the process more streamlined so that the action could be completed without leaving the page.
